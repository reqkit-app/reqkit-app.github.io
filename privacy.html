ReqKit

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

ReqKit is a native HTTP client for testing and debugging APIs. This policy describes how the application handles data. The short version: ReqKit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Data Collection

ReqKit collects no data of any kind. The application does not include analytics, telemetry, crash reporting services, or any form of tracking. No information about you, your device, or your usage is ever sent anywhere.

Network Requests

ReqKit sends only the HTTP requests you explicitly create and trigger. All network activity originates from your own input. The application makes no background connections to external servers.

Local Storage

Request collections, environments, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your machine and is not accessible to FDSoftware or any third party.

Third-Party Services

ReqKit does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics services.

Children's Privacy

ReqKit does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version of ReqKit, the updated policy will be published at this URL with a revised date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to fdsoftware.support@gmail.com.
